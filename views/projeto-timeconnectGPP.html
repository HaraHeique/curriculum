Time-Connect PPM

About Project

It consists of a private project, where the owner is Time-Now Engenharia, which is a company that promotes activities and services related to project management processes.

All Time-Connect software development takes place at AEVO (where I work as a developer), a Time-Now company that works to promote IT solutions focused on project management and other areas.

The software is focused on the Project Portfolio Management (PPM) service, which consists of management of a collection of projects in order to ensure compliance with the objectives of organizations.

The main idea is that in a single environment it is possible to gather all the management data of the projects, and it is possible that all stakeholders can access, from anywhere and anytime, the same information in an accurate and reliable way.
